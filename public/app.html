‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Intel Core i5 and i7?

+

Intel Core i7 processors generally have more cores, higher clock speeds, and larger cache compared to i5. This translates to better performance for intensive tasks like video editing, 3D rendering, and heavy multitasking. For everyday use, an i5 is usually sufficient.

Is 8GB RAM enough in 2025?

+

8GB is the minimum for basic tasks, but 16GB is recommended for most users. If you do video editing, gaming, or run many applications simultaneously, go for 16GB or 32GB.

Should I buy extended warranty?

+

It depends on the brand and your usage. Premium brands like Apple, Dell XPS, and ThinkPad have good build quality. Extended warranty is worth it for expensive laptops (above RM 5,000) or if you travel frequently.

# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## Introduction

AI Bradaa ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

This policy complies with Malaysia's **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, name, password (hashed)
- **Preferences**: Laptop preferences, search history, favorites
- **Communications**: Messages sent through AI Command chat

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Performance Data**: Load times, error logs (anonymized)

### Information NOT Collected

We **DO NOT** collect:
- Payment information (we don't sell anything)
- Precise location data
- Biometric data
- Social security or IC numbers
- Third-party tracking cookies

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Provide personalized laptop recommendations
   - Save your preferences and favorites
   - Deliver AI chat responses

2. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Fix bugs and optimize performance
   - Develop new features

3. **Communication**
   - Send magic link login emails
   - Notify you of service updates (if opted in)
   - Respond to support requests

### PDPA Compliance

We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: You explicitly consent when creating an account
- **Legitimate Interest**: To provide and improve our service
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with Malaysian law

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

- **Database**: Hosted on Netlify (US-based infrastructure)
- **Local Storage**: IndexedDB in your browser (device-only)

### Security Measures

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Password Hashing**: bcrypt with salt (never stored in plain text)
- **Access Control**: Limited employee access, audit logs
- **Regular Backups**: Encrypted backups with 90-day retention

### Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account Data | Until account deletion |
| Chat History | 90 days |
| Analytics | 30 days (anonymized after) |
| Error Logs | 30 days |

## Your Rights (PDPA)

Under Malaysia's PDPA, you have the right to:

### Access
Request a copy of your personal data

### Correction
Update incorrect or incomplete data

### Deletion
Request deletion of your account and data

### Withdraw Consent
Stop processing of your data (may limit service)

### Complaint
Lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commissioner
